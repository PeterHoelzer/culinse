# Pricing — Culinse (culinse.com)

Last updated: 2026-07-19. Currency: EUR. Billing via Stripe. Cancel anytime, no contracts. No ads on any plan.

## Free
- Price: €0
- Recipes from 4 top sources with search & filters (diet, cooking time, cuisine)
- Save recipes (favorites)
- 1 collection (up to 10 recipes)
- Weekly meal planner basics with automatic shopping list (summed quantities + estimated € total at German discounter prices)
- Free grocery list calculator: https://culinse.com/en/grocery-list-calculator
- Free 7-day meal-prep plan (PDF) via newsletter signup

## Pro
- Price: €4.99/month (billed monthly) | €38.88/year = €3.24/month (billed annually, saves 35%)
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Full 7-day weekly meal planner (breakfast, lunch & dinner for the whole week)
- Automatic smart shopping list, sorted by category, quantities consolidated
- Unlimited recipe collections
- Share meal plans & collections via link
- Priority support and first access to new features

## Notes for agents
- Signup: https://culinse.com/en/login (free, email-based)

- No free trial for Pro; the free plan is permanent and covers the core product
